Assignment 3: ePortfolio and Module Reflection

Link to Submission: ePortfolio ePortfolio live site (you're here!) Link to Submission: Module Reflection

The final assessment in this module is an e-portfolio that collects all the evidence of your work in this module. The overall assignment requires 2500 words equivalent worth of effort to complete it. 1000 words are for the required reflection.

Your e-portfolio should consist of:

  • Your answers to and outputs from exercises carried out during the module, including all your coding scripts and system designs. Aim to showcase at least 1 artefact from each unit. (Application of Knowledge and Understanding weighted at 10%, Independent working weighted at 10%)
  • Your analysis of the data/information gathered in the module, as well as information from applicable contributions to the collaborative discussion forums (Application of Knowledge and Understanding weighted at 10%)
  • Your evaluation of your final project in weeks 7 and 11 (Independent working weighted at 10%).
  • Reflection on (Criticality weighted at 40%).
    • Object-Orientated Programming based on what you have learned in this module.
    • Your work in the module.
    • The impact on your professional/personal development.

Your e-portfolio content should be presented in a structured, logical way.

Learning Outcomes:

  • Appraise and evaluate critically the concepts and principles of information systems.
  • Design or modify and document an object-oriented information system using appropriate tools.
  • Develop an object-oriented information system design, implementing this knowledge in applicable programming languages, such as Python and SQL.
  • Develop, implement and evaluate critically information system solutions to facilitate business decisions.
