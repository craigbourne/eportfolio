Unit 11: Software Engineering Project Management: Future Trends

In unit 11, we discussed a number of trends in project management, software engineering, computer science and IT which could drive and shape future directions of the discipline. We discussed how these trends may affect SEPM and drive future directions. 

Required Reading

  • Forsgren, N. et al. (2021) The SPACE of Developer Productivity - ACM Queue.
  • Khan, A. A. and Le, D-N. (2022) Evolving Software Processes: Trends and Future Directions. Hoboken, New Jersey: John Wiley & Sons. Chapters 9, 14 and 17.
  • Fahmideh, M. et al. (2023) 'Engineering Blockchain-Based Software Systems: Foundations, Survey, and Future Directions', ACM Computing Surveys, 55(6), pp. 1–44.
  • Laroui, M. et al. (2021) 'Edge and Fog Computing for IoT: A Survey on Current Research Activities & Future Directions', Computer Communications, 180, pp. 210–231.

ASSIGNMENT: Individual Task - Presentation

The focus in this unit was on the second assessed deliverable, which carried on from the team task in unit 6. The assignment was to produce a response to the changing conditions and challenges in delivering the EC/Synputer machine.

The response consisted of a presentation to major stakeholders that included a status/progress update, plus a plan and budget showing the changes required to mitigate new challenges.
