Unit 6: pytest and Test-Driven Development

In unit 6, we examined test-driven development in Python using pytest . We also considered the role and responsibilities of a User Experience researcher and the impact of emotional reactions on user experience.

Required Reading

  • Brooks, P. (1975) The Mythical Man-Month - Essays in Software Engineering. Anniversary ed. Addison-Wesley. Chapters 1-5.
  • Krekel, H. et al. (n.d.) Pytest: helps you write better programs. Pytest.
  • Giacomelli, J. (2020) Modern Test-driven Development in Python.

ASSIGNMENT: Team project - Synputer Project

The main task of the unit was the submission for the team task which consisted of a project report and an appendix.

We were provided with a transcript of a stakeholder conversation so we could analyse and extract the requirements of the project. We had to critique the requirements and identify any assumptions and gaps that could impact the successful delivery of the project.

We needed to produce a proposal that detailed a suitable development methodology (e.g. waterfall or agile based) for the computer system under consideration, supported by a discussion that justified the choice of methodology. We also needed to produce a set of requirements, a development plan with suitable milestones, proposed deliverables and an estimated cost of the system.

Our team met several times over the course of six weeks to complete the task. Minutes of those meetings can be found here.
