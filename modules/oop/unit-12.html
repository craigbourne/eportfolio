Unit 12: Working with Design Patterns to Structure Code

This unit focused on practical application of design patterns, particularly examining the factory pattern implementation. The content explored how multiple design patterns can be effectively combined within a single development project.

Required Reading

  • Philips, D. (2018) Python 3 Object-Oriented Programming, Chapter 10
  • Gamma, E., Helm, R., Johnson, R. & Vlissides, J. (1995) Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software
  • Zhang, C. & Budgen, D. (2012) What Do We Know about the Effectiveness of Software Design Patterns?
  • Kampffmeyer, H. & Zschaler, S. (2007) Finding the Pattern You Need
  • Guo, P. (2021) Ten Million Users and Ten Years Later: Python Tutor's Design Guidelines
