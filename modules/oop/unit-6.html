Unit 6: Abstract Methods and Interfaces

This unit explored the concept of interfaces in Python and their benefits in programme design. The content examined how interfaces support Python code design and considered metrics for evaluating the effectiveness of object-oriented programmes.

  • Gamma, E., Helm, R., Johnson, R. & Vlissides, J. (1995) Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software, Chapter 1
  • Kumar, A. & Panda, S.P. (2019) A Survey: How Python Pitches in IT-World
