Unit 5: Interviews, Survey Methods, and Questionnaire Design

In Unit 5, we examined interview techniques as a qualitative method and survey methods as a quantitative approach. We explored the difference between questionnaires (sets of questions) and surveys (the complete data gathering and analysis process), alongside pre-testing and post-testing methods that provide before-and-after comparisons.
