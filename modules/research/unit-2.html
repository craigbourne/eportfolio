Unit 2: Research Questions, the Literature Review and the Research Proposal

In Unit 2, we took a deeper dive into the risk management process, specifically looking at the effect of different types of assessment (Qualitative vs. Quantitative). We also practiced threat modelling and management, guided by the Threat Modelling Manifesto and the Mitre ATT&CK database.

Required Reading
