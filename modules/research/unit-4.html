Unit 4: Case Studies, Focus Groups and Observations

In Unit 4, we learned about industry and enterprise security standards. We discussed the role of standards in risk assessments and security designs and also the implications of standards for business continuity and disaster recovery.

Required Reading

  • Viegas, V. and Kuyucu, O. (2022) IT Security Controls: A Guide to Corporate Standards and Frameworks. 1st Edition. 1st edn. New York: Apress. Chapters 2 and 3
  • Barafort, B., Mesquida, A.-L. and Mas, A. (2019) 'ISO 31000-based integrated risk management process assessment model for IT organizations', Journal of Software: Evolution and Process, 31(1), p. e1984. DOI: 10.1002/smr.1984
  • Alhazmi, O. and Malaiya, Y. (2013) 'Evaluating Disaster Recovery Plans using the Cloud', 2013 Proceedings Annual Reliability and Maintainability Symposium (RAMS), 1(1), pp. 1-6.
  • Data Protection Commission. (2020) Case Studies: Data Protection Commission.
