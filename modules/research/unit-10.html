Unit 10: Research Writing

In Unit 10, we were introduced to research writing and how to structure a dissertation. We looked at the various sections in a dissertation and how to approach writing each component. The second assignment, a research proposal presentation, was due in this unit.

Required Reading

  • Dawson, C. (2015) Projects in Computing and Information Systems: A Student's Guide. Harlow: Pearson. Chapter 8.
  • Mayyas, F. and Alzoubi, K. (2022) Awareness and knowledge of manuscript writing and research integrity: A cross sectional survey among graduate students.
  • Stewart, O.G. (2023) Using digital media in the classroom as writing platforms for multimodal authoring, publishing, and reflecting.
  • Heng, L. (2011) 'Qualitative Research on Educational Technology: Philosophies, Methods and Challenges', International Journal of Education, 3(2).
  • Heard, S. (2016) The Scientist's Guide to Writing. Princeton, N.J.: Princeton University Press.
  • Jeffrey R. Wilson. (2021) Academic Writing. Harvard University.
  • Texas AM University. (no date) Research Papers.
