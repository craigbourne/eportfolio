Unit 3: Methodology and Research Methods

In Unit 3, we explored research design, distinguishing between exploratory research (where problems are not clearly defined) and conclusive research (focused on verifying insights). We examined the three main research methods: qualitative, quantitative and mixed methods, alongside primary and secondary data collection approaches.
