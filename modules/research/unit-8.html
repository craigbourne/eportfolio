Unit 8: Data Analysis and Visualisation

In Unit 8, we shifted focus to data analysis and presentation. We explored how qualitative data analysis differs from quantitative approaches, including coding responses and categorising data. We also examined data visualisation techniques and business intelligence dashboards for presenting findings in user-friendly ways.

Required Reading

  • Berenson, L., Levine, D. and Szabat, K. (2015) Basic Business Statistics: Concepts and Applications 14th Edn. Pearson.
  • Microsoft. (2023) Why data dashboards are important.
  • Elsevier Research. (no date) The The why and how of data visualization.
  • FT. (2023) Charts that work: FT visual vocabulary guide.
