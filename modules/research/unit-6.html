Unit 6: Quantitative Methods - Descriptive and Inferential Statistics

In Unit 6, we were introduced to quantitative methods and descriptive statistics. We explored how to summarise numerical data using measures of location (finding typical values) and measures of dispersion (understanding variability and spread), alongside graphical summaries for presenting data effectively.

Required Reading

  • Berenson, L., Levine, D., and Szabat, K. (2019) Basic Business Statistics: Concepts and Applications. 14th Edn. Pearson. Chapter 2 p 75-112. Chapter 3 p 152-184.
