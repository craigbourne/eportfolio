Unit 9: Validity and Generalisability in Research

In Unit 9, we examined validity, generalisability and reliability as three dimensions that affect research conclusions. We explored how reliability refers to consistency of measurement, whilst validity focuses on whether the measurement targets the correct thing, with both concepts needing consideration before data collection begins.
