Unit 12: Project Management and Managing Risk

In Unit 12, we examined project management and risk management. We were introduced to project life cycles and methodologies, exploring how projects can be impacted by risk and uncertainty. We learned how to develop risk management plans and change management processes to maintain project quality whilst minimising impact on cost and timelines.

Our final assignment was due in this module. We were asked to produce a reflection piece covering our learning journey through the module using Rolfe's model of What? So what? Now what?
