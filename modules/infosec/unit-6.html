Unit 6: The Great Debate - What Will be the Most Influential Trend in ISM in the Next 5 Years?

In Unit 6, we debated the question “what will be the most influential trend in SRM in the next 5 years?”. This was be based on the reading material and lecturecast from Unit 5, plus entries submitted to the course Wiki.

Our final assignments were also due in this unit. We were tasked with creating a python application that would act as a continuation of the risk report we created in Assignment 1. The appication should be able to produce a visualisation of an attack tree and for values to be added to the nodes. This would help the shop manager to understand the monetary impact of any threats identified.

Lastly, we were asked to produce a reflection piece covering our learning journeys through the module using Rolfe's model of What? So what? Now what?
